Shall we Begin

Exploring

There is somthing magical about getting outside and doing challenging things with the people you love. It always seems to bring beautiful bonds. Maybe it is the fact that it birngs people away from the world and often leads to deeper conversations. Whatever it is I am a big fan, and love capturing those moments to remind me in later dates the power of simplyfing and disconnecting from the world to really connect with others.

The Gospel

The temple is one of the most calming places for me, and in the beauty of every temple I go to I find such a beautiful parellel to the magnificwent beauty that the gospel brings to the lives of everyone. That is somthing that I absolutely love to capture. I love to capture the light that I see in peoples eyes and people in moments of true joy because it all loops back for me. It all loops back to the why, which is Christ. He brings joy and light and life to the world! The temple exudes that. I hope that my life and the content that I capture and create can too.

Family

My family is such a valuable part of my life. Each member has played such a valuable role in my life, and shaped who I am. My number one goal in life is to have a family of my own one day.

Events

I thrive in social events. Absolutly love them. I love the planning, inviting, and execution of it all. I think that connecting with others is one of the coolest parts of this life. Putting on events facilitates those connections !
